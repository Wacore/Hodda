FEATURED PROJECT

HomeZone

HomeZone is a Pomodoro timer app with different features. Its purpose is to make it simple for all the new users transitioning to working from home to find easy access to tools that help them stay productive. Additionally, the second goal is to educate users on some beneficial health and psychological key points such as taking breaks, stretches, desk ergonomics, and more.
